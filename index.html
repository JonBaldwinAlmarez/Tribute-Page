President Barack Obama

The First Black American

Greatest achievements

  • Ended war on Iraq - He ended war on Iraq after he took office and the last troops on Iraq left in December 2011.
  • Healthcare Reform - Pioneered healthcare reform by passing affordable care act.
  • Death of Osama Bin Ladin - During his presidency the leader of the terrorist organization al-Qaeda Osama Bin Ladin was killed in an special operation.
  • Recovery From Great Recession - He help millions of people recover from the great recession by passing American Recovery and Reinvestment Act.
  • Support for veterans - He increase the budget for Department of Veterans Affairs helping millions of retired heroes.
  • Climate Change - During his presidency he spend $ 90 billion for green energy.
  • Banking Reform - The collapse of wall street is due to the abusive practice of the banking sector the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act make sure that such mistake never happend again.
